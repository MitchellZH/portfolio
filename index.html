About Me.

Hello! I'm a full stack developer currently living in Spokane, Washington. Some of my passions are learning new technologies, working collaboratively to make quality products and finding solutions to complex problems. I really enjoy the art of programming and am always striving to perfect my craft. My goals are to keep learning new technologies, keep improving on my current skill set and to continue to grow a great reputation through the quality of my work.
